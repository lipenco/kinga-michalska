Hi!

My name is Kinga Michalska.I am a photographer and a traveller from Poland.

I graduated form Warsaw School of Photography in 2010 and from Cultural Studies at Warsaw University in 2012. I use my camera to tell other people's stories, to show their emotions and to make them notice the beauty that I see in them.

I travelled and photographed in India, Nepal, Colombia, Peru, Bolivia, Ecuador, Canada, US, Eastern and Western Europe. Wherever I go I like to meet ordinary people, take pictures of them, their families, their everyday life. I love to work with children, because of the honest and spontaneous way in which they behave.

I am particularly interested in the themes of femininity, body and movement. I treat photography as a magical tool which allows me to meet interesting people and to create unique bounds.
